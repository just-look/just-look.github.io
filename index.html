Features

Personalized Services

Save time and money by finding services that are personalized to your family. Our engine finds you the most relevant results.

Forum

Connect with other families as well as professionals to get quick answers to your questions. Sentero's platform allows you to be part of a community.

Budgeting

Managing your finances can be stressful. Our budgeting tool can help your family get a better understanding of your spending habits.

Ask a Professional

Our network of professionals answer your specific questions and prevent the spread of incorrect information.

Clinical Trial Matching

Based on your profile, our platform can recommend your family suitable clinical trials and devices.

High Quality Content

Our partner clinics publish high quality, relevant content to help inform your decisions.
